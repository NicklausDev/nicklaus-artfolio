POTRAITS

01

Vibrant Feminity

This commission was to take the submitted selfie and transform it into a picture that showcased "I am female and I will be be energitcly bold". Thus the employ of vibrant splashes of color.

Purple Hearts

This was undoubtedly my best pencil, graphite potrait. It may not look like it but these were two different photos from the couple's best moments drawn into one. Beautiful, it spots a subtle purple overlay since that is their favorite color.

Eternal Love

This potrait represents so much in the sense that there's a lot of ways to view the world, the most beautiful approach being it's built for two, sharing each other's love, forever... plus it was my first ever digital potrait commission.

UI/UX

02

Piquant Spices

The design for the launch website for Piquant Spices, a spice blend retailer based in Kenya. The site was to encompass their branding in a way that looked modern and clean.

KODN

An ERP system supposed to make business management easier to perform, the UI had to be easy to understand and navigate through in order to aliviate management problems so owners could just focus on their businesses.

Krayo Africa Onboarding

Part of a team tasked to bring forward the Krayo art platform. I was tasked to make the onboarding process smooth and fun. Thus broke it down to segments and used vibrant colors with subtle art in the backgrounds.
